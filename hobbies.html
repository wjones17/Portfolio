My Hobbies

Building and Flying FPV Drones

Over the past few years, I have mastered the art of building and flying FPV (First-Person-View) drones. These drones allow you to experience flight from the drone's perspective through a pair of video goggles. The sensation is exhilarating, and the drones can reach impressive speeds, depending on personal modifications. For those interested in building their own FPV drones, I highly recommend getFPV.com. This site provides a wealth of resources and components for building drones, including ready-to-fly options. Another valuable resource is "MR.
